Diamond Aircraft Group Austria has integrated Volocopter, enabling the electric air mobility company to reduce costs, retain its workforce, and focus on certification milestones. The reorganization strengthens Volocopter's position in urban air mobility while expanding Diamond's portfolio into sustainable aviation.
