Experienced air rescue pilots from ADAC Luftrettung operated the VoloCity eVTOL aircraft in real-world conditions using a VR-assisted SimX ground station for the first time. The testing provided insights into usability, pilot-aircraft interaction, and the potential for integrating eVTOL aircraft into existing rescue operations.
