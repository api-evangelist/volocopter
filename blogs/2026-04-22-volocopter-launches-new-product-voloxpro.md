Volocopter unveiled the VoloXPro, an electrically powered ultralight multicopter designed for both recreational flying and professional passenger transport. The aircraft features eighteen rotors, accommodates two passengers, and combines aviation-grade safety standards with a modular design to keep costs competitive.
